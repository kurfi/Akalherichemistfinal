AK ALHERI CHEMIST - USER MANUAL & OPERATING GUIDE
==================================================

Welcome to the AK Alheri Chemist POS System. This manual guides you through every feature of the application.

1. GETTING STARTED
------------------
- Login:
  - Open the application.
  - Enter your username and password.
  - Default Admin Credentials (Change immediately in Settings):
    - Username: admin
    - Password: password
- Roles:
  - Admin: Full access to all features (Inventory, Reports, Settings).
  - Cashier: Restricted access (POS, Customers, Dashboard).

2. DASHBOARD
------------
- The Dashboard provides a quick overview of your business performance.
- Key Metrics:
  - Total Sales (Today): Sum of all completed transactions.
  - Transactions: Number of sales made.
  - Low Stock Alerts: Products running low.
  - Expiring Batches: Items expiring within 90 days.
  - Recent Activity: A log of the latest sales.

3. POS (POINT OF SALE) SYSTEM
-----------------------------
The POS screen is where you process daily sales.

A. Processing a Sale:
   1. Search Product:
      - Click the search bar (top-left).
      - Type a product name or scan a barcode.
      - Click the product in the list to add it to the cart.
   2. Adjust Quantity:
      - In the Cart (right panel), use "+" or "-" buttons to change quantity.
      - Click "Remove" to delete an item.
   3. Discount:
      - Enter a discount amount in the "Discount" field at the bottom of the cart.

B. Payment Methods:
   - Click "Pay Now".
   - Select Method:
     - Cash: Standard cash payment.
     - POS / Card: Payment via bank terminal.
     - Transfer: Bank transfer confirmation.
     - Multipay: Split payment (e.g., part Cash, part Transfer).
     - Debt / Credit: Assign sale to a registered customer's account (Customer must be selected first).

C. Hold & Resume Sale:
   - If a customer forgets an item, click "Hold" (Pause icon).
   - The cart clears for the next customer.
   - To Resume: Click the "Held" tab in the right panel and select "Resume".

D. Receipts:
   - After payment, a receipt preview appears.
   - Click "Save PDF" or "Save Image" to save the receipt to your computer (Documents/AK Alheri Chemist/Receipts).
   - Click "Print" (if printer is connected) or use the system print dialog.

4. INVENTORY MANAGEMENT (Admin Only)
------------------------------------
Manage your product stock and pricing.

A. Adding a Product:
   - Go to "Inventory".
   - Click "Add Product".
   - Enter Name, Category, Price, and Minimum Stock Level (for alerts).
   - Click "Save Product".

B. Adding Stock (Batches):
   - Find the product in the list.
   - Click the "Plus" (+) icon next to the stock count.
   - Enter Batch Number, Expiry Date, Quantity, and Cost Price.
   - Click "Add Batch".
   - *Note: The system uses FIFO (First-In-First-Out). It deducts from the oldest batch first during sales.*

C. Editing/Deleting:
   - Click the "Edit" (Pencil) icon to change product details.
   - Click the "Delete" (Trash) icon to remove a product (only if no sales history exists).

5. CUSTOMER MANAGEMENT
----------------------
Track loyal customers and manage debts.

A. Adding a Customer:
   - Go to "Customers".
   - Click "Add Customer".
   - Enter Name, Phone, and optional details.
   - Click "Save".

B. Managing Debt:
   - In the Customer list, view "Current Debt".
   - To record a debt payment:
     - Click the "Wallet" icon next to the customer.
     - Enter the amount paid by the customer.
     - Select payment method (Cash/Transfer).
     - Click "Confirm Payment". The debt balance will decrease.

6. RETURNS MANAGEMENT (Admin Only)
----------------------------------
Process customer returns and refunds.

A. Processing a Return:
   - Go to "Returns".
   - Click "Process New Return".
   - Enter the Sale ID (found on the receipt) to load the transaction.
   - Select the items being returned.
   - Choose Condition:
     - "Restock": Item is good and goes back into inventory.
     - "Damaged": Item is bad and is recorded as a loss.
   - Select Reason (e.g., Defective, Wrong Item).
   - Click "Process Refund".

B. Viewing History:
   - The Returns page shows a log of all past returns.
   - Click "View Receipt" to see details of a specific return.

7. EXPENSES (Admin Only)
------------------------
Track shop expenditures.

- Go to "Expenses".
- Click "Add Expense".
- Enter Description (e.g., "Fuel for Generator"), Category, Amount, and Date.
- Click "Save Expense".
- View the list to track daily/monthly spending.

8. REPORTS & ANALYTICS (Admin Only)
-----------------------------------
Generate insights on business performance.

- Go to "Reports".
- Select Date Range: Today, Week, Month, Year, or Custom.
- Tabs:
  - Overview: Revenue, Profit Est., Top Products charts.
  - Sales Details: List of every transaction.
  - Inventory Health: Low stock and expiring items list.
  - Financials: Expense breakdown.
  - Return Analytics: Refund stats and loss analysis.
- Export:
  - Click "PDF Report" (top right) to generate a detailed business report.
  - File saves to: Documents/AK Alheri Chemist/Reports.

9. SETTINGS (Admin Only)
------------------------
Configure the system.

A. User Management:
   - Add new staff accounts (Cashier or Admin).
   - Reset passwords or delete users.

B. File Save Locations:
   - Click folder icons to change where Receipts, Reports, and Backups are saved on your computer.
   - Default: Documents/AK Alheri Chemist/...

C. Data Backup & Restore:
   - **Export Backup:** Click to save a full copy of your database. Do this Weekly!
     - Save location: Documents/AK Alheri Chemist/Exports.
   - **Restore Backup:** Use this ONLY to recover lost data. It overwrites current data with the backup file.
